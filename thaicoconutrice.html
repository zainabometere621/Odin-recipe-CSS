Thai coconut rice recipe

Odin Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of uncooked jasmine rice
  • 1 cup of coconut oil
  • Half cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon Thai red curry paste
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce
  • Chopped scallions for garnish

Preparation (steps):

  1. Heat oil in pan and fry curry past
  2. Add rice and stir to coat with oil and curry past
  3. Add cocnut milk, water, and fish sauce
  4. ring to boil, then simmer until cooked

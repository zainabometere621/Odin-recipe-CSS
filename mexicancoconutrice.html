Mexican Coconut Rice Recipe

Odin Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of uncooked rice
  • 1 cup of coconut oil
  • Half cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Preparation (steps):

  1. Heat oil in pan and fry onion and garlic
  2. Add rice and stir to coat with oil
  3. Add cocnut milk, water, lime juice, and cumin
  4. ring to boil, then simmer until cooked
